# Chai Books and Marketing — Newsletter

A marketing newsletter exploring brand strategy, content marketing, and creator economy trends through the lens of books and literary culture. Published on LinkedIn and Substack.

## What This Newsletter Is About

Chai Books and Marketing sits at the intersection of two things I know well: literary culture and marketing strategy. Each edition breaks down a real marketing trend and connects it to something the publishing world already figured out — because books have been the original content marketing for centuries.

## Topics I Cover

- Why organic social media is not dead (and how to prove it with data)
- What romance novel marketing teaches us about product launches
- The anti-ambition era in beauty branding and what it means for consumer behavior
- Personalization at scale vs. radical specificity for smaller audiences
- Community-led growth lessons from BookTok and Bookstagram
- How publishers build 6-month launch campaigns (and what B2B brands can steal from them)

## Why This Newsletter Exists

I built Chai Books and The Moon, a literary community of 8,000+ followers, entirely through organic content. No paid ads. That experience taught me things about community, brand building, and audience psychology that I now unpack in this newsletter — backed by data, case studies, and real examples.
